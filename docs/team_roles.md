# Team Roles

## Person 1 — Project Leader / GitHub Manager
Owns README, GitHub organization, final upload, and submission.

## Person 2 — Legal Evidence Researcher
Owns answer key, sources, and legal explanations.

## Person 3 — AI Prompt Tester
Owns questions, prompts, and raw ChatGPT/Grok answers.

## Person 4 — Scoring Analyst
Owns scoring, final results, and score chart.

## Person 5 — Judge / Presenter
No laptop needed. Reads the courtroom intro/verdict, keeps time, and ensures every team member speaks in the video.
