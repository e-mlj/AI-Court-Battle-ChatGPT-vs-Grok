# Slides Outline

## Slide 1 — Title
AI Court Battle: ChatGPT vs Grok

## Slide 2 — Problem
LLMs can sound confident while being wrong, especially in legal topics.

## Slide 3 — Dataset
65 total questions and 5 live false-premise questions.

## Slide 4 — Method
Same prompt for both models. Ground truth from public legal sources and metadata sources.

## Slide 5 — Scoring
2 points per question: correct yes/no + false premise explanation.

## Slide 6 — Results
Full dataset:
- ChatGPT: 130 / 130
- Grok: 116 / 130

Live set:
- ChatGPT: 10 / 10
- Grok: 10 / 10

## Slide 7 — Conclusion
Model agreement is not proof. Legal claims require public-source verification.
